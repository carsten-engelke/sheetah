# Plants
This is a list of plants that might grow on my balcony.

## 1. Petunias
Colorful summer flowers that thrive in sunny spots and bloom throughout the season.

- **Water needs:** water regularly, never let the soil dry out completely
- **Sun exposure:** full sun to partial shade
- **Lifespan:** annual plant (summer bloom)

## 2. Geraniums
Hardy and reliable, perfect for sunny balconies with minimal maintenance.

- **Water needs:** moderate watering, allow soil to dry slightly between waterings
- **Sun exposure:** prefers full sun
- **Lifespan:** perennial, often kept in pots over multiple seasons

## 3. Pansies
Cold-resistant flowers ideal for spring and autumn on German balconies.

## 4. Lavender
Fragrant purple flowers that love sunlight and well-drained soil.

## 5. Impatiens
Shade-tolerant flowers with vibrant blooms, great for partially shaded areas.

## 6. Hedera (Ivy)
Climbing plant that adapts well to various light conditions and adds greenery.

## 7. Tomatoes (Cherry)
Compact varieties grow well in pots on sunny balconies during warmer months.

## 8. Herbs (Basil, Parsley, Thyme)
Practical kitchen herbs that grow quickly and thrive in containers.

## 9. Calibrachoa
Delicate trailing flowers resembling petunias, perfect for hanging baskets.

## 10. Hostas
Shade-loving foliage plants with attractive leaves, ideal for north-facing balconies.
